A Comprehensive Basic
course Obstetrics and Gynecology course

The course is going to be conducted on 25th and 26th March 2022. The course will provide theoretical knowledge and basic skills needed on ultrasound in obstetrics and gynecologist the course is ISUOG approved. By the end of it a certificate of attendance shall be provided to the delegates. Additionally the candidate will get free one year ISUOG membership which includes discount to ISUOG future courses and high quality education and web resources. The course is supported by Legacy clinic - Rwanda
